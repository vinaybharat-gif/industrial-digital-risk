Who We Are

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and artificial intelligence risk. We are dedicated to building the next generation of risk practitioners for highly regulated, high-consequence environments.

By uniquely bridging the gap between deep academic insight—partnering closely with leading UK universities—and real-world, hands-on projects, we ensure our graduates are equipped not just with theoretical knowledge, but with actionable resilience strategies for today's threat landscape.

Our Core Model

Academy

Intensive bootcamps and comprehensive training programs designed for university students and transitioning professionals, delivering hands-on skills in digital risk and compliance.

Innovation & Incubation

A sandbox for developing proprietary IP, pioneering future risk models, and establishing robust AI governance frameworks to tackle tomorrow's vulnerabilities.

Advisory Services

Expert consulting that helps established organizations implement and audit against critical frameworks, including NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.
